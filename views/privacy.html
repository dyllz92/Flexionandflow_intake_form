Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2026

Introduction

Hemisphere Wellness ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our intake form and feedback application.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal information (name, email, date of birth)
  • Health and wellness information (massage preferences, health concerns)
  • Feedback and session notes
  • Account authentication data

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our massage and wellness services
  • Personalize your experience
  • Process your intake forms and feedback
  • Communicate with you about your sessions
  • Maintain account security and prevent fraud
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our services

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Please contact us to exercise these rights.
